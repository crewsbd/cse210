                                HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS
    ARROW KEY: Move cursor
    ENTER KEY: Select an option
PLAY ORDER
    1. Select an encounter card.
    2. Choose to play the encounter.
        A. If you fight, choose from aquired single-use items.
        B. The player might take damage.
        C. If the player wins, they get a reward item.
    3. Choose to reject the encounter
        A. Some encounters have punishments for rejecting.
        B. You get no reward.
    4. The next player takes their turn.
VICTORY CONDITIONS
    1. If all but one player are eliminated, that player wins.
    2. If on player defeats the Boss, that player wins.
